**Assignment overview**

This assignment asked us to revisit our seqing sampler and create a circuit with two gaps with an LED which lights up when the circuit's closed. To create the two gaps, I used conductive material on one side with a paper clip with a gap in between which closes when I bend the paper clip to touch the conductive material. The other gap is created by two chucks of satin stiching with conductive thread which closes when manually pressed together. 

**Tip or trick**
Make sure to leave some extra room when making the two bars of satin stiching so that the thread doesn't accidently touch and complete the circuit.
