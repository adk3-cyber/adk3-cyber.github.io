**Assignment overview**
This assignement requires us to use the temperature and light sensors on the arduino to detect weather or not it's dark and or cold. If it's dark and cold then all the red leds turn on to warn that ghosts are near. 

**Tip or trick**
Make sure the off board detector is properly connected becuase if it's not exactly flat it won't work.
