**Assignment overview**
This assignment requires us to make a circuit made of alligator clips attached to the arduino. When the circuit is open the buzzer on the arduino uses morse code to write my name Arjun and when closed it plays SOS.

**Tip or trick**
Make sure to really pay attention to the length of the delays,otherwise you might end up with just a long continous buzz.
