**Assignment overview**
This assignment requires us to make use of different frequencies on the buzzer to play a song. I chose happy birthday because it's an easy song to program and I had some issues integrating other peoples' more complex code. 

**Tip or trick**
Look up the frequencies for different notes becuase then you can just copy and paste all of them at the top and make it easy to integrate them into your code.
