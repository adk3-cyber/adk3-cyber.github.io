**Assignment overview**
This assignment requires us to make a circuit out of alligator clips. When the circuit is open, the buzzer will play my name, Arjun, and if the buzzer is closed it will play SOS. 

**Tip or trick**
Really pay attention to how the functions for each letter and then the combanation of them work, especially the spacing. I messed up the spacing and it was just kind of one continuous buzz.
